Quarto unifies & extends the RMarkdown ecosystem

A new open-source scientific and technical publishing system. The goal is to make the process of creating and collaborating on scientific and technical documents dramatically better.

Illustration:

Julie: Quarto unifies & extends the RMarkdown ecosystem. It is a new open-source scientific and technical publishing system. It builds on 10 years of insight from RMarkdown - Inputs in multiple languages - Outputs in multiple formats - All going through the same machinery
